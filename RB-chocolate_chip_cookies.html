Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

  • 2 sticks softened butter
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup packed light brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 and 1/4 cups all-purpose flower
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 cups milk chocolate chips 
 Beat butter, granulated sugar, brown sugar, and 
 vanilla extract into a large bowl until creamy.
 Add eggs to mixture.
 In a seperate bowl, stir together flour, baking
 soda, and salt. Gradually stir into butter 
 mixture.
 Stir in chocolate chips.
 Drop teaspoon sized clumps onto ungreased cookie
 sheet.
 Bake for 8-10 minutes at 375 degrees F.
 Makes roughly 5 dozen.
