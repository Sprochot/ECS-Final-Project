Banana Bread

Ingredients

  • 1 1/3 cups sugar
  • 2/3 cup softened butter
  • 4 eggs
  • 6 tablespoons sour milk or buttermilk
  • 2 cups mashed, overripe bananas
  • 4 cups sifted flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt 
 In a bowl, mix eggs, butter, and sugar thoroughly.
 Stir in sour/butter milk along with the bananas.
 Stir in flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
 Pour mixture into greased loaf pans and let sit 20 
 minutes before baking.
 Bake at 350 for 50-60 minutes.
